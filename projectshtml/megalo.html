Megalomalady

As a student artist and developer, I was able to work on a team under MassDigi, a team comprised of students from both Becker College, and the Rhode Island School of Design. Together, we worked on and are on the verge of publishing Megalomalady, a fun mobile game that will be released on the IOS store.


Along with that, we were more recently contracted by a company called Earth Echo to make a re-skinned version of Megalomalady, which will be more geared towards an environmentally aware focus, so our fan base can have fun, while acknowledging the serious message behind our game.


I worked under the Art Director, as both an icon concept artist, and as an icon game asset artist. More or less, I was both the creative mind, and the artist that produced and executed some of the icons in both Megalomalady, and our Earth Echo re-skin. Here are examples of those icons below!
